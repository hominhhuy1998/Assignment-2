History of Fish Tastic

Fish Tastic was founded in 2010 by Ho Minh Huy, also, the co-sponsor of Singapore Sea Aquarium. Fish Tastic not only sell a fishes but we also provide information, documents and articles about the beauty of marine life. With the help of Ushaka Marine World and The Okinawa Churaumi Aquarium, Fish Tastic has been achieved Singapore outstanding enterprise 2016 and Singapore Successful Brand 2016-2017

We have a large sale trading partners worldwide. We continue to explore marine life and look for partnership around the world. Our most successful method that leading our company to the top of the world is “We need to understand our clients”. We never stop looking and learning new information about marine life and we will continue to bring our company to customers best and fastest way.

We are here to share our knowledge and make your hobby as easy as possible. We believe when people truthy want to explore and learn we will try our best to get you to there.
